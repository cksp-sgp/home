ABOUT CKSP ENGINEERING

Welcome to CKSP ENGINEERING, your premier destination for all your door frame and related repair needs in Singapore.

With years of expertise and a dedicated team of professionals, we take pride in delivering top-quality services to ensure the safety and functionality of your doors and windows.

Pricing

We offer affordable price rates, which are among the most competitive. Our experienced technicians give professional work, ensuring that you receive the best value for your money.

Quality

Accurate & Detailed Inspection. Our technicians will provide you with a detailed evaluation of your door and the area to determine what is causing the issue.

Efficiency

We Will Recommend The Most Cost-effective Solution For You. Our experienced technicians can troubleshoot and resolve any issues that may arise with your doors quickly and efficiently.

Warranty

All of our work is backed by a warranty, and we guarantee the quality of our repairs. We only use the highest-quality parts and materials to ensure that your doors are reliable and secure.

Accreditation

At CKSP Engineering, we pride ourselves on our commitment to safety and quality. We are proud to announce that we are BizSafe3 certified, demonstrating our adherence to stringent safety standards and practices.

In addition to our BizSafe3 certification, we are also recognized as a BCA approved contractor. This accreditation from the Building and Construction Authority (BCA) further solidifies our reputation for excellence in the construction industry. 

With our BizSafe3 certification and BCA approval, our clients can have confidence in our ability to provide safe, reliable, and high-quality services for their construction and engineering needs.

At CKSP Engineering, safety and quality are paramount, and we are committed to delivering exceptional results on every project.
